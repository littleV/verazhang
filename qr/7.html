Have you heard of a Skinner's box?

A Skinner's box is a psychological experiment. It's a box full of pigeons where the experimenter randomly drop food to the inside of the box. After a while, the pigeons developed a variety of behaviors with the belief that these behaviors can bring them food. For example, they dance. You can search on the internet to read more.

So I painted this, a pigeon break out of a skinner box, and actually see the real world.

I think life is the same, you'll never know the real world unless you break the box around you.
