5 REASONS WHY YOU WILL LOVE THE GOLDEN TRIANGLE TOUR

Source

If you have a less time and you are planning to visit India, we have a perfect plan tailored for you. Come and enjoy the golden triangle tour, to the three pillar cities of India considered in the terms of tourism.If you visit these three cities, you will have a taste of the whole country.

Let us have a look at the reasons why we will love the golden triangle tour:

THE FORTS AND MONUMENTS:

All the three cities are a basket full of monuments, forts and palaces. When on a golden triangle trip we can visit The Taj Mahal, Jantar Mantar, Amber Fort, India Gate, Red Fort, Jal Mahal, Hawa Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri, Qutub Minar, Safdarjung Fort, and City Palace. While we will visit these places, we will see the diversified nature of the country. It has been ruled by various kings of Rajutana Dynasty, Mughal Dynasty, Lodi Dynasty, Khilji Dynasty and many- many more. Plan a trip and visit these places to see how gorgeously India is been decorated by these huge beauties.

THE MOUTHWATERING CUISINE

India from its yester years is the spice capital of the country, the food our country has to offer will spice up your tongue. We have varied cuisines like mughali, rajasthani, sindhi, gujrati, south- indian, Punjabi, Bengali and a lot more. Delhi being the capital of the state serves you all the cuisines in one, but ssshhh… forget this and give a try to the street food of the city and your taste buds will thank you. Agra on the other hand is famous for its sweet dish named, pethas, which come in a number of flavours like Pan, rose, saffron and many more. Jaipur has in stored for us the great taste of the Rajasthani dishes. Do not miss the ‘daal baati churma’ and ‘balushahi’. Visit India and gain some pounds with the tasty food we have to offer.

BLEND OF MODERNITY AND HISTORICITY

India yet considered in the list of developing countries is showing its rapid growth with the tall buildings, smooth roads, schools, colleges and what not. These cities prove to be the perfect blend of modernity and historicity with monuments, forts and old heritage properties. As they say, “blend the two goods to make the best” these two are a perfect blend, which makes a place easily accessible and fun to live in.

Tourists love these cities because the people living locally in these towns understand what a tourist is saying and can help them, plus who doesn’t like to visit the beauties of the past.

THE DIVERSIFIED CULTURAL TICKLE

The person visiting India knows how valuable our cultural values are. These three cities will give us a tickle of cultures and religions like Hindu culture, Marwari culture, Islamic culture, Punjabi culture, rajasthani culture and a few more. We will be seeing the monuments and pilgrimage centres from all the cultural background in these cities, taste the food from different culture. When you come for a golden triangle tour, you will be tickled with different values and cultures all together in a secular India.

BAGFULL SHOPPING

In all of the three cities in the tour circuit of the golden triangle, we will be encountering amazing shopping places for different kinds of stuff from all the places. You can buy souvenirs for the people near and dear to you. From these places, we can buy stuffs like mojiri, bandhani print, leather bags, carpets, blue pottery, inexpensive clothing, miniature paintings and portraits and a lot more.

Famous markets in Delhi are sarojini nagar, Connaught place, Delhi haat. From Agra we have one that is the Sadar bazarFree Web Content, do grab some Agra shoes popular in all over the world. In the pink city do visit the Johari bazar for some boho and traditional jewellery and also take a walk to Bapu Bazar for anything and everything Rajasthani.
